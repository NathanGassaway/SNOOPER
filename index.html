• SNOOPER publishes new attitudes around spatial constructions. It is organic and open in its format in a sense that different approaches are asked to contribute to each issue. The first issue looks at interdependent structures: stretches of tunnels and conditions of control and movement. A penetration hole, something that connects, escapes and irritates within a given structure. It contains questions on indirect domination, friendship and isolation.
